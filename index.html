CampaignWallet

About Our Project

The project aims to improve transparency in campaign finance information.

The Problem

This information, generally speaking, is inaccessible to the average voter and the politicians have little to no incentive to improve its accessibility.

The Solution

Our chrome extension allows users to hover over the names of members of Congress on any website and see a popup providing campaign finance statistics.

How to Use CampaignWallet Extension

Installation

Download or clone our GitHub repository

Load the extension into your browser. This extension works for all Chromium based browsers. Instructions are provided below for Google Chrome and Microsoft Edge. Other supported browsers include Samsung Internet, Opera and many others.

Chrome

In your Chrome browser's address bar, head to chrome://extensions/. Find the developer mode toggle in the top right corner and activate Developer mode. Towards the top left corner, click the Load unpacked button.

Edge

In your Edge browser's address bar, head to edge://extensions/. Find the developer mode toggle in the left side menu, second from the bottom, and activate Developer mode. In the right pannel, to the right of the bolded words Installed extensions, click the Load unpacked button.

Navigate to the folder where you have your files to upload that folder. Now upload the `dist` folder. Our extension should now be uploaded.

Demonstration

When reading online, if a dotted line appears below a senator or representative's name, you can hover there to see a pop-up on their campaign finance data.
